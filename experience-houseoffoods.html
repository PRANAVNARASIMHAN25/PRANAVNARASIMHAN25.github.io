// approach

How I Approached It

I owned the product from zero — there was no existing playbook. I started by defining the target persona, mapping the buyer journey, and identifying key decision points where the product needed to create trust and urgency.

On the growth side, I used customer data and trend analysis to continuously refine the product portfolio. This meant testing different positioning, price points, and packaging to find the sweet spot between mass appeal and premium perception.

The website was treated as both a storefront and a growth lever — each page was designed with conversion intent, supported by analytics to validate what was working and where users were dropping off.
