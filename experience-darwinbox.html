How I Approached It

I started by mapping the existing manual ticket triage workflow end-to-end — identifying where bottlenecks occurred, which routing decisions were repetitive, and where human error was highest.

Rather than simply connecting Jira to Darwinbox, I designed a rule-based routing framework that could scale with changing team structures and priorities. The system uses lookup tables for user resolution and conditional decision trees for smart routing.

I worked closely with the support and engineering teams in agile sprints, iterating on the routing logic and SLA thresholds to ensure the system handled edge cases while being maintainable for non-technical stakeholders.
